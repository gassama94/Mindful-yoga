Welcome To Mindful Yoga

Who are We ?

Our aim is to provide you with the tools and resources to help you improve your mind and body. We offer instructional videos, articles, and online classes to deepen your understanding of yoga and its benefits. Our expert teachers and writers are here to inspire and empower you to embark on your own personal journey of self-discovery and growth. Our unique selling point is our collaboration with small-scale boutique hotels, which allows us to provide a personalized and intimate experience. Our retreats are flexible, allowing you to choose your own starting dates and number of days to suit your schedule and needs. Whether you're looking to improve your physical fitness, deepen your yoga practice, or simply unwind, our team of experienced professionals will guide you every step of the way. Healthy Tips

About Yegu Sauna

Yegu sauna therapy has numerous health benefits, including reducing the risk of cancer, increasing energy and hair growth, and promoting longevity. Use is typically 3-4 times per week.

HBOT - a key to Longevity

Hyperbaric oxygen therapy increases oxygen to the body's tissues, aiding healing, reducing inflammation, and enhancing overall well-being. It extends lifespan by stimulating stem cell production.

The 3 things to improve your health

Regular exercise, Balanced diet and Stress management are essential for maintaining good health, preventing chronic diseases and improving mental well-being.

Change your mindest & your future.

Auto-hypnosis, is a technique that uses hypnosis to change behavior and beliefs. It helps with weight loss, stress, self-esteem, confidence, and performance in sports.

Go no Sugar!

A lot sugar can lead to weight gain, diabetes, heart disease, tooth decay, cognitive decline and Alzheimer's disease. Avoid eating too much sugar, choose healthier options, limit sugary drinks and read nutrition labels.

Why do Yoga.

Yoga benefits health by reducing stress, improving flexibility and strength, balance, sleep, lung capacity, mental health, chronic pain, focusing and concentration and increasing self-awareness and self-esteem.
